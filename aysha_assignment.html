What is Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing is the use of social media to market a company's products and services.Social media marketing provides companies with a way to reach new customers,engage with existing customers and promote their desired culture,mission or tone. Also known ass"digital marketing"and "e-marketing",social media marketing has purpose-built data analytics tools that alllow marketers to track how successful their efforts are.

Breaking Down Social Media Marketing

Social media websites allow marketers to employ a broad range of of tactics and strategies to promote content and have people engage with it.Many social networks allow users to provide detailed geographical,demographic anfd personal information,which wuill enable marketers to tailor their messages to what is mostly likely to resonate with users.

Social Media Marketing Strategy

A major strategy used in social media marketing is to develop messages and content that individual users will share with their family, friends and c0-workers.This strategy relies on word of mouth and provides several benefits.First,it increases the message reach to networks and users that a social media manager might not have been able to access otherwise.Second,shared content carries an implicit endorsement when sent by someone the recipient knows and trusts.
