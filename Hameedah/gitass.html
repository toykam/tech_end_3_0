WHAT IS SOCIAL MEDIA?

Social media is computer-based technology that facilitates the sharing of ideas, thoughts, and informations through the building of virtual networks and communities..... Users engage in social media via computer, tablet or smartphones via web-based software or web application, often utilizing it for messaging.
